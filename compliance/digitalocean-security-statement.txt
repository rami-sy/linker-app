Written Statement — Hosting Provider Security Certification
Linker App (linker.land)
Date: May 2026

Hosting Provider: DigitalOcean, LLC

DigitalOcean holds the following certifications that cover physical security and secure media disposal controls, as evaluated by a third-party auditor:

1. SOC 2 Type II
   - Auditor: Independent third-party auditing firm
   - Controls covered: CC6.4 (Physical Access Controls) and CC6.5 (Secure Media Disposal)
   - Reference: https://www.digitalocean.com/trust/certification-reports

2. ISO/IEC 27001:2013
   - Controls covered: A.8.3 (Media Handling), A.11.1 (Secure Areas), A.11.2 (Equipment Security)
   - Reference: https://www.digitalocean.com/trust/certification-reports

All Platform Data received from Meta is stored exclusively on DigitalOcean infrastructure, which is covered by the above certifications.
