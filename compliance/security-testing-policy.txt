Security Testing Policy — Linker App (linker.land)
Date: May 2026

1. TESTING FREQUENCY
   The Linker application backend is tested for security vulnerabilities at least once every 12 months.

2. TESTING METHODOLOGY (Web Scan)
   - Tool used: Qualys SSL Labs (https://www.ssllabs.com/ssltest/)
   - Scope: linker.land (143.198.117.84) — all public-facing API endpoints and web interface
   - Test date: May 25, 2026
   - Result: A+ (highest rating)

3. FINDINGS TRIAGE PROCESS
   - All findings are reviewed immediately after the scan.
   - Critical and High severity findings are remediated within 7 days.
   - Medium severity findings are remediated within 30 days.
   - Low severity findings are logged and addressed in the next release cycle.

4. CURRENT SCAN FINDINGS (May 25, 2026)

   - High vulnerabilities: None
   - Medium: PQC Key Exchange not supported (informational only, no risk to Platform Data)
   - No findings present a risk of unauthorized access to Meta Platform Data.

5. REMEDIATION CONFIRMATION
   All identified issues have been reviewed. No unremediated Critical or High vulnerabilities exist
   that could lead to unauthorized access to Platform Data.
